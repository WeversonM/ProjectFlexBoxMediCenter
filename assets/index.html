Diverticulitis:

Diverticulitis is an inflammation of the diverticula. Generally, episodes of acute diverticulitis are usually not complicated. Uncomplicated diverticulitis refers to localized inflammation, while complicated diverticulitis refers to inflammation associated with an abscess, phlegm, fistula, obstruction, bleeding, and perforation.
